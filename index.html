🌿 Green Machines: How Plants Keep Us Alive

The hidden power of plants 🌎

🌱 Introduction

Plants are essential to life on Earth. They provide oxygen, food, and support ecosystems. Without plants, life would not exist.

🌍 Why Plants Matter

Oxygen: Plants produce oxygen through photosynthesis.

Food: Plants are the base of the food chain.

Wildlife: Animals depend on plants for shelter.

Climate: Plants absorb carbon dioxide and help fight climate change.

🌿 Bryophyte: Moss

Scientific Name: Bryophyta

Mosses grow in damp areas and absorb water through their leaves.

🌿 Pteridophyte: Fern

Scientific Name: Pteridophyta

Ferns reproduce using spores instead of seeds.

🔚 Conclusion

Plants are essential for life. We must protect them for the future.
