🌿 The Science Behind Plants

🌱 Plant Physiology

Plant physiology studies how plants function. Through photosynthesis, plants convert sunlight into energy using chlorophyll. They also transport water and nutrients through specialized tissues called xylem and phloem.

🧬 Plant Genetics

Plant genetics explains how traits are passed down. DNA controls characteristics like leaf shape, growth speed, and resistance to disease. Scientists use plant genetics to grow stronger and more productive crops.

🌍 Plant Ecology

Plants play a major role in ecosystems. They are producers, meaning they create energy for all other organisms. Plants also form habitats and help regulate climate and water cycles.

🤝 Human-Plant Connections

Humans rely on plants for food, medicine, and materials. Many medicines come from plants, and agriculture depends on understanding plant science.
